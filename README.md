# RS-Flipper

Your smart flipping assistant for the Grand Exchange in Old School RuneScape.

RS-Flipper tells you what to flip, at what price, right now — and tracks every
trade automatically. Open the GE, follow the suggestion, collect the profit.

## Features

- **Live buy/sell suggestions** tuned to your cash stack and your target fill
  time, with transparent reasoning (margin, estimated fill time, gp/h) for
  every pick
- **Full order guidance**: the plugin walks you through placing, adjusting,
  aborting and collecting offers — including keybind prefill for price and
  quantity
- **Automatic profit tracking** across all your characters, with session
  stats, gp/h and a full flip history (also on your web dashboard)
- **Real-time dump alerts**: when someone panic-sells far below market value,
  the plugin flashes and pre-fills the buy — you only confirm
- **Settings sync** across devices

## Requirements

RS-Flipper is powered by a server-side suggestion engine. A free account
(created at [rs-flipper.com](https://rs-flipper.com) or via Discord login
inside the plugin) is required. **During the launch period every feature is
free, with unlimited characters.**

To generate suggestions and track your flips, the plugin sends your current
Grand Exchange offers, trade fills, inventory coin/item context and game name
to the RS-Flipper API (`api.rs-flipper.com`). Characters are stored under a
pseudonymous hash. Details: [Privacy Policy](https://rs-flipper.com/legal/privacy)
and [Terms](https://rs-flipper.com/legal/terms).

RS-Flipper only makes suggestions. It never automates gameplay, clicks or
inputs of any kind.
